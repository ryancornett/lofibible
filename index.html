Offering the King James makes sense...

But why the Berean Standard Bible?

The Berean Standard Bible (BSB) is in the public domain and licensing is not required. The BSB is free to share--there is no limit to how many verses/chapters may be distributed or what percentage of a work the words of the BSB may make up.

Since 60% of the world's languages do not have a translation that may be freely shared, such openness with God's Word should be appreciated and encouraged.

Why Lofi Music?

Lofi music is a popular choice for background ambiance while reading or listening. It is by definition soothing, simple, and unobtrusive. Lofi typically features mellow beats and gentle rhythms, creating a calming environment that helps listeners concentrate without distraction. The minimalistic and easygoing nature of lofi can enhance focus and promote thoughfulness, which is particularly beneficial when engaging with the Bible which often requires reflection and contemplation.

Lofi can also mask ambient noise and create a more serene and private reading or listening experience. This can be especially valuable in today's often noisy and hectic environments, where finding a quiet space can be challenging. The gentle, instrumental sounds help to block out distractions, allowing for a deeper immersion into the richness of the Scriptures.
