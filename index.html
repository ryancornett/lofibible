Lofi Audio Bible

Why the Berean Standard Bible translation?

The Berean Standard Bible (BSB) is in the public domain and licensing is not required. The BSB is free to share--there is no limit to how many verses/chapters may be distributed or what percentage of a work the words of the BSB may make up.

Since 60% of the world's languages do not have a translation that may be freely shared, such openness with God's Word should be appreciated and encouraged.

Even the Bible audio is made available freely to whomever wants it.

Why Lofi/Chill Beats?

This relaxing genre is a favorite backing track for quite a few folks as they go about their day and work or relax around the house. Now you can do all that with the Holy Word of God a part of it too.

And who knows? Maybe someday soon thie site can branch out to include classical music, Celtic tunes, or Bluegrass.
